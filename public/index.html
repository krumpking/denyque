About Us

DenyQue Projects

Founded in 2019, Denyque project Specialises in manufacturing and supply of quality equipment. We also trade consumable products to fulfill their needs at the right price. We leveraged our industry knowledge and the notion that we need to create an attitude of self-reliance, accountability, and discipline of creating a self-sufficient system.

Denyque projects are aimed at helping Zimbabwe and African countries towards self-sufficiency and self-reliance in the manufacturing and supply of quality products and services, by establishing efficient production plants in strategic locations close to vital economic zones

Mission

Denyque projects is aimed at helping Zimbabwe and African countries towards self sufficiency and self reliance in the manufacturing and supply of quality products and services ,by establishing effecient production plants in strategic locations close to key economic zones

Vision

To build the next generational leadership team and be perceived to be a leader in the manufacturing sector and it's industries.

Values

Integrity and transparency, Customer satisfaction Excellence , Work Smart- collaboration ,Respect
